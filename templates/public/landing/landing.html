Important Deadlines for Paper Submission

  • Paper Submission Deadline

    Dec 1, 2024

  • Notification of Acceptance

    Jan 27, 2025

  • Final Version Submission

    Feb 27, 2025

Submission Guidelines

  • Language: Only manuscripts in English will be accepted.
  • Length: Submissions should be between 10 to 18 pages, following the publisher’s format.
  • Criteria: Papers will be evaluated based on originality, relevance, and technical soundness.
  • Content: Manuscripts must clearly identify objectives, scientific questions, or hypotheses, supported by a theoretical base, research methodology, and discussion of results.

Review Process

  • Each paper will undergo a double-blind review by at least two independent reviewers.
  • Max 3 papers per reviewer.
  • Submissions will be checked for plagiarism using tools like CrossCheck.

Review Criteria

  1. Empirical base and practical applicability
  2. Clear identification of objectives and hypotheses.
  3. Theoretical foundation and robustness
  4. Research methodology
  5. Results presentation and discussion
  6. Scientific or practical novelty
  7. Relevance to the conference topics
  8. Papers must have less than 20% similarity with other works to be accepted.
